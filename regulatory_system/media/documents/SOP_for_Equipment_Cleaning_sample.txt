**Title:** SOP for Equipment Cleaning

**1. Purpose**
To define the procedure for cleaning medical equipment to ensure safety and hygiene.

**2. Scope**
Applicable to all equipment used in the production and testing areas.

**3. Responsibilities**

* QA Team: Ensure compliance
* Operator: Perform cleaning as per SOP

**4. Procedure**

1. Switch off the equipment.
2. Disconnect from power supply.
3. Clean surfaces using approved disinfectant.
4. Dry using clean cloth.
5. Record the cleaning activity in logbook.

**5. Precautions**

* Do not use excessive liquid.
* Wear gloves while cleaning.

**6. Records**
Cleaning Log Sheet

**7. Revision History**
