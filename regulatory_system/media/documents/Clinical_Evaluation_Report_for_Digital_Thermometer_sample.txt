**Title:** Clinical Evaluation Report for Digital Thermometer

**1. Objective**
To evaluate the clinical performance and safety of the digital thermometer.

**2. Device Description**
A digital thermometer used to measure human body temperature.

**3. Clinical Data**

* Accuracy: ±0.1°C
* Response Time: 10 seconds
* Tested on 50 subjects

**4. Risk Assessment**

* Low risk device
* No serious adverse events reported

**5. Conclusion**
The device is safe and performs as intended.

**6. References**

* ISO 13485
* ISO 14971

**7. Approval**
Approved by QA Department
