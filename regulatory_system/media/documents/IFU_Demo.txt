**Title:** IFU for Digital Thermometer

**1. Purpose**
This device is intended to measure human body temperature accurately.

**2. Instructions for Use**

* Switch on the thermometer by pressing the power button.
* Place the tip under the tongue or armpit.
* Wait until the beep sound is heard.
* Read the temperature displayed on the screen.

**3. Safety Precautions**

* Do not use the device if it is damaged.
* Keep out of reach of children.
* Clean the device before and after use.

**4. Storage Conditions**

* Store in a cool and dry place.
* Avoid direct sunlight.
